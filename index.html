About Department

Our Mission is to partner with Communities in the twin-city of Bhubaneswar and Cuttack, to enforce the law without fear or favour, secure justice for victims of crime, maintain peace and public order and create a sense of security among people. Towards this objective we will focus on professionalism, adoption of technology and integrity always.

It is my proud privilege to lead Police Commissionerate in Twin Cities of Bhubaneswar and Cuttack. Commissionerate encompasses prestigious Capital City of Bhubaneswar and millennium City of Cuttack. Growing urbanization, establishment of eminent educational institutions, spread of service industries have led to vast expansion of the twin City. The core challenges before Police Commissionerate remain maintenance of law and order, prevention and detection of crime, especially organized crime, Traffic management, VIP security.
